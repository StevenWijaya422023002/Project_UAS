Call of Duty: Warzone Mobile is an upcoming first-person shooter game developed and published by Activision. It was announced in September 2022. In November 2022, the beta version launched in Australia. Currently the beta version is available in Australia, Chile, Norway, Sweden and Germany. The beta version received generally positive reviews from Tom's Guide's Marshall Honorof and IGN's Emma Matthews. Recent developments have indicated a delay in the official release of Call of Duty: Warzone Mobile, as reported by Eurogamer.The revised timeline now points to a scheduled deployment in spring 2024. This delay has been met with some disappointment among fans, however many others view that it underscores Activision's commitment to delivering a polished and optimized mobile gaming experience, potentially setting a new standard within the genre. Engadget provided perspectives on the anticipation surrounding the game and speculated on the reasons behind the strategic delay.

Call of Duty: Warzone Mobile is known to feature 120 player lobbies with a confirmed set of game modes consisting of "Battle Royale" and "Resurgence". Both game modes consist of "Solo", "Duo's", "Trio's", and "Quad's" lobby types for both types of game modes.
