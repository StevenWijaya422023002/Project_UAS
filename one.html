It's your turn to become a hero for fun in this highly-anticipated, action-packed, and immersive game set in the world of the hit anime series, One Punch Man - ONE PUNCH MAN: WORLD. Follow the journey of Saitama, a Hero for Fun. Play the game, play the anime. Defeat the Subterranean King, Mosquito Girl, Beast King, and many other familiar bosses from the series.

CONTROL THE ACTION
Customize your action fighting style with each character's unique set of abilities, skills, and cinematic finishing moves in satisfying combat, where you can feel every punch.
EXECUTE JUSTICE
Follow the journey of Saitama, a Hero for Fun, and defeat powerful villains like the Subterranean King, Mosquito Girl, Beast King, and many other familiar bosses from the series.
CHALLENGE TOUGH MONSTERS WITH FRIENDS
Take on missions cooperatively with friends through the Hero Association HQ hub and beat your opponents together.
A WHOLE WORLD TO EXPLORE
Feel like saving the day at the Hero Association, or playing a mini-game at the Arcade, or even shopping for favorite goodies at the supermarket? Play the way you want to play and explore where you want to explore in this immersive world based on the One Punch Man anime series.
