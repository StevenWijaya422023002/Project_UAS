About This Game

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare (CoD:MW) is a first-person shooter game that was released in 2007, and it has been played by many people since then. It was developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. The game has a lot of different modes that can be played in single player or multiplayer mode. In multiplayer mode, there are many different game types that can be played such as team deathmatch, capture the flag, domination or search and destroy. The single player mode has different missions that need to be completed before you can progress to the next mission.

The best thing about this game is its graphics. They are very realistic and give you an amazing feeling when playing the game. The sound effects are also very good and make it feel like you are actually there in real life fighting against other players on your team or against other enemy teams as well as fighting against AI bots in story mode missions too!
